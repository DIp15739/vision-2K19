Improve your technical skills and enhance your problem solving abilities

About

This event will help you to improve your technical skills and enhance your problem solving abilities. It will challenge you with coding skills.

✓ Round 1: Tech-Cross
->In this round the candidates will have to solve a crossword given to them which will be based on technical words.

✓ Round 2: The one with the missing pieces
->In this round the candidates will have to complete a jigsaw puzzle by collecting pieces of the puzzle through answering the questions.

✓ Round 3: Code-Hunt
-> In this round the candidates will have to solve certain cryptic words with help of keys which will act as password for next questions and then finally solve a error in a C program to be declared as a winner.

Rules

• Decisions taken by faculties and coordinators will be final .
• Participants are not allowed to use cell phone during the event.
• Any violations in the rules will result in disqualification.
• Basic knowledge of C is required for the 3rd round.
