About

Team up in duo,escape the hell challenges and ultimately chiken out your partner.

✓ Round 1: BLIND RETRIEVER
-> This round would be performed by two players. Pair of different symbols will be arranged across the class. Among two players, one will be blind folded and other player will guide the first player to make pairs of the symbol in a minute.

✓ Round 2: HOUSE OF CARDS
-> In this round, participants have to build a pyramid using cards. In the second last card clue will be given. Using this clue, they have to find the final card. They will be judged according to time taken by the participants to perform the round.

✓ Round 3: SCAVENGER’S HUNT
-> In this round, one partner will hold bricks and another will find the way to escape the maze. Partners are allowed to exchange their task for three times. Judging criteria will be decided according to the time taken by them. At last, there will be surprise game among the winning team.

Rules
