Fusion

About

This event gives you a real life experience about how to do Fusion

✓ Round 1: THRASH-THROW
-> This round would be in pair of 2. One will be blindfolded and has to throw balls to the other person who will catch the balls in a glass. Judging criteria will be time limit of 1 minute.

✓ Round 2: PICK , TIE , REPEAT
-> This round would be in a pair of 2. There legs will be tied with the rope individually. Here, some cards are tied on a rope and first person has to go jumping towards the card and untie it and come back to its original place, then the other person will go and do the same task to tie the cards. Judging criteria will be time based.

✓ Round 3: FORTUNE HUNTER
-> This round would be in a pair of 2. Firstly, they have to scan QR code & decode the puzzle given in it. Then they have to reach at that location where our volunteer will be present. He/She will give him the task which they have to perform. Judging criteria will be time limit.
